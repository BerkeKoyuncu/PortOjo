Scan Blackout (Freeze) Window

Define a global time window during which manual scans are blocked and scheduled scans are deferred. This prevents resource overload and network disruption during peak business hours.

Time Comparison Rules

  • Times are compared against the system timezone: Europe/Istanbul (TRT).
  • Overnight windows are supported automatically (e.g. 22:00 to 06:00 will block scans overnight).
  • Manual scans triggered during the blackout will show an error alert.
  • Scheduled scans due during this time will be deferred automatically until the window ends.
